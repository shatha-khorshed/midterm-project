About me

I am currently pursuing a diploma in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College and have maintained a GPA of 3.7/4.0. I am expected to graduate in August 2019. I have also spent four years studying Graphic Design at the Lebanese International University in Yemen.

Through my studies, I have gained experience in designing, developing, and prototyping contents for both print and digital formats including website layouts, website UX/UI, print work, and brand and identity work. As well, I have gained experience in using wireframing and mockups to express my design ideas, and I have developed knowledge in using Content Management Systems (CMS) such as Drupal, WordPress, and Joomla.
